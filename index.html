CEO_CHARTER.md

CEO Charter

Scope: Final authority on strategy, capital allocation, and executive hiring. Accountable to the board for company performance.

Key Metrics: Revenue growth rate, burn rate, team retention, quarterly OKR completion.

Decision Authority: Approves all spending >$10,000. Hires/fires direct reports. Sets quarterly priorities.

HIRING_SOP.md

Hiring Standard Operating Procedure

Step 1: Hiring manager opens a role request with justification and budget impact.

Step 2: Department head reviews and approves within 48 hours.

Step 3: Finance confirms headcount budget. Role posted within 24 hours of approval.

BUDGET_TRACKING.md

Budget Tracking Template

Monthly Runway: Cash on hand ÷ monthly burn rate. Update weekly.

Budget Categories: Payroll, infrastructure, marketing, tools/services, legal, contingency.

Alert Threshold: If runway drops below 4 months, trigger cost review with CFO and CEO.

INCIDENT_RESPONSE_SOP.md

Incident Response Procedure

Severity 1 (Critical): Service down. All hands. CTO takes command. External comms within 30 minutes.

Severity 2 (Major): Degraded service. On-call team investigates. Update every 60 minutes.

Post-Incident: Blameless retrospective within 48 hours. Action items tracked to completion.
